A Prophetic Blueprint For Global Communities

International Rescue Ministry operates as a dedicated prophetic ecosystem raised to standardizes deliverance processes, enforce spiritual restoration parameters, and initiate profound baseline breakthroughs.

By constructing operational networks across continental divisions, we offer secure environments for direct intercession, community building, and personal pastoral cultivation.

Apostle Silas Mabomba

The Visionary of the Ministry

Pastor Silas M Mabomba was born in 1974 in Kikwit, Kwilu Province, in the Democratic Republic of Congo. He holds two Master's degrees — one in Applied Pedagogy, Literature, and Human Sciences, and a second in Theology — and has served as a certified teacher for over twenty-five years. His approach to preaching, discipleship, and leadership training bears the mark of both the classroom and the altar.

His call to ministry came in September 1995 at a Christian revival congregation in the DRC. Over the following three decades, he served as exhorter, soul winner, preacher, and Director of Intercession for twenty-three years, before being formally consecrated and installed as titular pastor in August 2008.

In September 2017, believing the Lord was directing him toward the global expansion of his ministry in keeping with the Great Commission of Matthew 28:19–20, Pastor Kalonji relocated to the United States. He settled in Raleigh, North Carolina, where he was recognized, certified, and ordained as associate pastor at Encounter Christian Fellowship — and where a small Anglo-Francophone prayer cell he helped establish eventually grew into the International Rescue Ministry.

On June 11, 2023, IRM held its first official gathering. Through a series of visions and prophetic leadings received between 2017 and 2025, Pastor Kalonji was directed to expand the ministry online — bringing together believers from the United States, the Democratic Republic of Congo, and nations across the world in daily prayer, intercession, and fellowship.

He is a husband and father of six children, and a man deeply committed to agape love, humanitarian service, and the restoration of those who are lost or afflicted. His life's work is anchored in one conviction: that the needs he ministers to in his own community are mirrored among people far beyond it.
