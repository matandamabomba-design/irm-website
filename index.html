Our History

International Rescue Ministry is a ministry born out of a deep vision to bring hope, restoration, and salvation to the nations. We connect a global community across borders, uniting passionate believers in the United States, the DRC, and around the world to pray, worship, and serve together.

Learn More

One ministry, several extensions

To accomplish a great mission entrusted to us, International Rescue Ministry is expanding. Today, our communities of faith are actively deploying in the United States, as well as in provinces of the Democratic Republic of the Congo, notably in Kinshasa and Kikwit.
